About

Codespire is the first edition of the National Level Event to be organized under the aegis of Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research, Indore by the Google Developer Student Clubs, AITR in collaboration with the Association for Computing Machinery, AITR and Association for Computing Machinery Women, AITR

Codespire is the perfect platform for young innovative minds to showcase their potential talent of ideating to provide viable industry-ready solutions to real-world problems with a technical edge and the art of weaving creative solutions to some brainstorming problems of competitive programming through logical problem-solving skills. Our basic aim is to develop a platform for aspiring tech enthusiasts to showcase their ideas and coding skills.

F.A.Q

  • How much time will we get to work on the project?

    The participants will be provided with domains in which they can choose their own problem statements to which they have to lay out the solution. So, there will be no particular timeframe to work on the projects

  • I am not familiar with coding, can I still participate?

    Yes! - willingness to learn is all that is needed to participate, as learning and growing goes hand in hand. Desire to garner new skills is the first stepping stone towards success and hence your readiness to learn and gain knowledge will be your only intellectual investment for the contest.

  • Who can participate in the hackathon?

    Anybody and Everybody who are currently enrolled in University.

  • Is it compulsory to participate as a team?

    Yes! As teamwork is the secret that makes common people achieve uncommon success, also it's well known that alone we can do so little, but together we can do so much!

  • Will there be any registration costs?

    Yes! The registration amount will be 120/- per team.
